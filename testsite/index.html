The FikkA platform gets you better candidates with a faster TAT (Turn Around Time).

Via a single FikkA interface, companies can pick the best candidates either from their existing partners or by an expanded ecosystem of FikkA’s curated list of partners (known for their expertise in sourcing candidates with particular skills, experience and from specific industries). We also support you through the interviewing and closure cycle.

The back-end parsing and machine learning algorithms understand your requirements better over time. Since the “human touch” is equally important in getting suitable candidates, it is also part of the workflow.
