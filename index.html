How FikkA Works

Companies

The FikkA platform gets you better candidates with a faster TAT (Turn Around Time).

Via a single FikkA interface, companies can pick the best candidates either from their existing partners or by an expanded ecosystem of FikkA’s curated list of partners (known for their expertise in sourcing candidates with particular skills, experience and from specific industries). We also support you through the interviewing and closure cycle.

The back-end parsing and machine learning algorithms understand your requirements better over time. Since the “human touch” is equally important in getting suitable candidates, it is also part of the workflow.

Recruitment Partners

We work with Recruitment Partners by giving them access to the FikkA platform for resume parsing and intelligent filtering tools. We reduce their sales & marketing effort and keep it a win-win by ensuring complete transparency at all steps of our automated workflow. The result : a more predictable revenue model for our partners.

‘I had some apprehensions initially, but got quite comfortable with the FikkA platform fast. Appreciate their transparency.’ Recruitment partner in IT space

‘The FikkA tools help me clear the clutter from the heaps of resumes I get and fulfil more jobs faster.’ One of the leading Recruitment partners in Bangalore

Candidates

Candidates would have updated their profiles across different job portals. FikkA ensures that the initial match of a candidate profile to a specific job description is automated (so that you do not miss out on potential opportunities). At the same time, a recruiter would reach out to you via email/call to make sure that there is a profile fit – and once we have your approval, your profile would be sent to the client. Over the coming months, we plan to give candidates access to the entire workflow so that you can not only keep your profile updated, but also have complete transparency as to what stage your profile is with different companies.

Do note that FikkA would get your profile from existing databases/ portals – so you do not have to upload your profile here again!
